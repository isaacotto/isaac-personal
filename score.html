Claytosmunda Claytoniana (2023) --- for unaccompanied piano

"Interrupted" fern --- a very old species. All others in its genus are now extinct, as far as we know. Launched my dissertation concert in 2023.

Modular No. XV (2023) --- for bass clarinet + effects, trombone, and trap kit

The fifteenth in a series of short pieces meant to be smashed up, broken apart, and put back together. Please forgive the shoddy {O-G} orthography---this was a draft prepared in very short order in advance of a concert.

High Structure Carbon Black (2023) --- for solo horn

A short solo (after Braxton) illustrating one way we might hybridize {O-G} with traditional notation.

Video here.

W/M (2023) --- for winds, 'cello, bass, and trap kit

Inspired by a large, grimy warehouse holding detritus to be sorted for recycling where I took several pieces of furniture.

Flexible ensemble piece intended to serve as an introduction to {Otto-Glyphs} by honing in on simple dots, lines, and curves---the system's quanta.

Nycticorax (2020, rev. 2021) --- for violin duo

A short duo composed for the very kind and generous Miolina in 2020 and revised in 2021. Deploys microtonality in a perhaps alarmingly un-rigorous fashion, but hopefully to pleasant timbral effect.

Video here.

DENSE STAR POLYMER CONJUGATES (2021) --- for unaccompanied 'cello

Composed for Bella Pepke's Masters recital in 2021. A spiritual homage to Gesualdo's "Tenebrae" featuring unsung libretto.
